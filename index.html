SIGNS THAT YOUR AIR CONDITIONER IS FAULTY
Here are major signs that your air conditioner is in need of repair and to stay ahead of that, please pay attention to the signs below:

No or inadequate Cool Air

when you notice that you air conditioner isn’t releasing enough or any cool air at all from the vents, this is usually and indication that something has gone wrong with the compressor or that the refrigerant level is low.

Foul Odors

Strong smells coming from an AC vent could be an indication of issues that can include; mold or mildew growing inside the units. It could also be caused by burned out wire that has to be immediately replaced..

Strange sounds

If you notice any kind of strange noise from your air conditioner, its an indication that there is something wrong.

Leakage

When you find moisture where it is not supposed to be around your air conditioner, it’s an indication that there could either be issue in the condensate line or refrigerant leak which could pose a lot of health hazard to the users.
